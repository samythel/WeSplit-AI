Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 12, 2026

WeSplit ("we", "our", or "the app") is a receipt-splitting application. This policy describes how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Receipt images: When you scan or upload a receipt, the image is sent to third-party AI services (Google Gemini, Gladia) for processing. We do not store your receipt images on any server. Images remain on your device and are only transmitted temporarily for analysis.

Voice recordings: When you use the voice feature, audio is recorded on your device and sent to Gladia for transcription. Recordings are not stored after transcription is complete.

No account required: WeSplit does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers.

Purchase data: If you subscribe to WeSplit Pro, payment is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment details.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Your display name (optional, set by you)
  • Scan history and receipt breakdowns
  • Subscription status cache
  • Monthly scan counter
  • Theme and language preferences

This data is never transmitted to our servers. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app.

3. Third-Party Services

WeSplit uses the following third-party services for AI processing:

  • Google Gemini API – receipt image analysis and voice-to-item matching
  • Gladia API – speech-to-text transcription

These services process data according to their own privacy policies. We send only the minimum data required (receipt image or audio recording) and do not send any personal identifiers.

4. Analytics & Tracking

WeSplit does not use any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies. We do not track you.

5. Children's Privacy

WeSplit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
